# 📖 AgentAide User Manual

Welcome to **AgentAide**, your premium workspace for managing real estate assets, tracking guest itineraries, and automating your daily client communication. 

This guide will walk you through the core features of the app so you can streamline your workflow from day one.

---

## 1. The Properties Hub

The Properties Hub is your main dashboard. It gives you a bird's-eye view of your entire real estate portfolio.

### Adding a Property
1. Tap the **`+`** button floating at the bottom right of the screen.
2. Fill out the core details: Name, Address, Description, Capacity, and whether it is listed on Airbnb.
3. Tap **Add** under the "Property Photos" section to select images from your device. The first image you select will automatically become the cover photo for that property.
4. Tap **Create Property**.

### Understanding Property Statuses
AgentAide uses an intelligent calendar system to automatically color-code your properties based on their actual real-world availability. A property can hold multiple statuses at the same time:

* 🟢 **Vacant:** No one is physically in the property *today*.
* 🔴 **Occupied:** A guest is physically in the property *today*.
* 🔵 **Reserved:** There is a future booking scheduled for this property. 

*Example: If a house is empty today, but a guest arrives next week, you will see both the **Vacant** and **Reserved** badges side-by-side.*

## 2. The Active Stays Hub

The Stays Hub tracks all incoming, active, and past guest itineraries. 

### Adding a New Stay
Tap the **`+`** button. You will be presented with three ways to log an itinerary:

1. **Auto-Parse Clipboard:** If a client emails or texts you their itinerary, copy the text to your phone's clipboard, open AgentAide, and tap this button. The app will automatically extract the flight number, dates, and guest count.
2. **Scan Document / Photo:** (See Section 3 below).
3. **Enter Manually:** Opens a clean form where you can link a property, select exact arrival/departure dates using the native calendar, set arrival times, and log Special Requests (e.g., "Early check-in needed").

### Managing a Stay
Tapping on any Active Stay card will open its Details panel. From here, you can view the linked property, flight data, and special requests. You can also tap the **Pencil** icon in the top right to edit the dates, or the **Trash** icon to permanently delete the log.

---

## 3. Smart Ticket Scanner (OCR)

To save you from manually typing out complex flight details, AgentAide includes a built-in AI document scanner.

1. Take a photo or screenshot of the guest's boarding pass or flight confirmation.
2. In the Stays Hub, tap **`+`** and select **Scan Document / Photo**.
3. Choose the image from your camera roll.
4. AgentAide will read the image, extract the flight number, calculate the correct arrival date and time, and instantly pre-fill your form. Just select the property and hit Save!

*Note: Document scanning requires an active internet connection to process the image.*

## 4. One-Tap Communication & Dispatch

AgentAide is designed to keep you connected with your clients and partners with zero friction.

### Generating a Cloud Dossier
When viewing a specific Property, tap the **Generate Cloud Dossier** button. 
AgentAide will securely upload your property photos and details to the cloud, generate a beautiful public web-link, and open WhatsApp so you can instantly send the portfolio to a prospective client.

### Dispatching Logistics
Inside any **Stay Details** panel, you will find two quick-action buttons at the bottom:
* **Client Dispatch:** Opens WhatsApp with a pre-formatted, warm welcome message containing the property address and check-in details.
* **Driver Dispatch:** Opens WhatsApp with a concise, logistics-focused message containing the flight number, arrival time, guest count, and destination property for your transport partners.

---

## 5. Offline Reliability

We know real estate happens on the go, often in places with poor cell reception. 

AgentAide was built as an **Offline-First** application. All of your properties, photos, and stays are securely saved directly to your device's internal storage. You can add properties, edit stays, and view your entire dashboard completely offline. 

*An internet connection is only required when scanning boarding passes or generating new Cloud Dossier web links.*
